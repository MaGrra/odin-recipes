Lasagna

About the dish

Lasagna is an italian dish, made form pasta plates, tomatoe souce and some other things. It takes about one hour to get ready and is vrty tasty

If you would like to make this dish at home just grab the things listed bellow and follow the instructions to get this thing started

Ingredients

  • Pasta plates
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Cheese
  • Grinded meat
  • Some spices
  • Onion and garlic

Steps:

  1. Wet the pasta for couple of minutes
  2. Put one layer in the pan
  3. Add meat, sauce, onion and cheese
  4. Repeat the layers till the tray is full
  5. Put a lot of cheese on top
  6. Cook in oven for 60 minutes

Cut it up in sqares for each serving. Remember the food is very hot so don't hurt yourself
