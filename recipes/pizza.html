Ingredients

  • Flower
  • Water
  • Spices
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Cheese
  • Sausage

Steps:

  1. Make dough using flower, water etc
  2. Let it chill for a bit
  3. Make about 0.3cm thick thing out of dough (does not have to bee round)
  4. Lay with tomatoe sauce and cheese on top
  5. Add sausage
  6. Bake for about 10 minutes
